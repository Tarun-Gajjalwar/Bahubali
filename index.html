Bahubali The Epic

Baahubali: The Epic is an upcoming Indian epic action film directed and edited by S. S. Rajamouli, serving as a re-edited and remastered single-film version of his earlier two-part Baahubali saga, Baahubali: The Beginning (2015) and Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017). The film is scheduled for theatrical release on 31 October 2025 in India, with screenings in IMAX, 4DX, D-Box, Dolby Cinema, and EPIQ formats. It is described as a combined narrative weaving together the grandeur and drama of both original films into one epic tale.
